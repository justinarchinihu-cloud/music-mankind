Album Bio

This record came about when my friend and producer, D’Andre Neal, messaged me about recording. He asked if me and my band wanted to come to Valencia College, where he works as a part of the studio’s staff. We set a date and I began to write some music.

Tyler wasn't able to make the session, so I decided we should just do it all trio. The tunes I wrote worked surprisingly well for trio playing.

I wrote all the tunes on this record besides one. Ethan Grussgott wrote the tune “Ronin”.

This record was also a sort of a farewell to friends and an era of life. Ethan, Justin, and I had spent the past three years learning how to play music together.
